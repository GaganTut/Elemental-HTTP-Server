Atomic number 11

Sodium is a Group 1 element (or IA in older labelling styles). Group 1 elements are often referred to as the %22alkali metals%22. The chemistry of sodium is dominated by the %2B1 ion Na%2B. Sodium salts impart a characteristic orange%2Fyellow colour to flames and orange street lighting is orange because of the presence of sodium in the lamp.
