Pure aluminium is a silvery-white metal with many desirable characteristics. It is light%2C nontoxic (as the metal)%2C nonmagnetic and nonsparking. It is somewhat decorative. It is easily formed%2C machined%2C and cast. Pure aluminium is soft and lacks strength%2C but alloys with small amounts of copper%2C magnesium%2C silicon%2C manganese%2C and other elements have very useful properties. Aluminium is an abundant element in the earth's crust%2C but it is not found free in nature. The Bayer process is used to refine aluminium from bauxite%2C an aluminium ore.
