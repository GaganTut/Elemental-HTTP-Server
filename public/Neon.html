Neon

Ne

Atomic number 10

Neon is a very inert element. Neon forms an unstable hydrate. In a vacuum discharge tube%2C neon glows reddish orange. Of all the rare gases%2C the discharge of neon is the most intense at ordinary voltages and currents. It is present in the atmosphere as 1 part in 65000.%0A%0ALiquid neon has over 40 times more refrigerating capacity than liquid helium%2C and more than 3 times that of liquid hydrogen.
