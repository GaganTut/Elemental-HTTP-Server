Fluorine

F

Atomic number 9

Fluorine is a Group 17 element. Fluorine is the most electronegative and reactive of all elements. It is a pale yellow%2C corrosive gas%2C which reacts with practically all organic and inorganic substances. Finely divided metals%2C glass%2C ceramics%2C carbon%2C and even water burn in fluorine with a bright flame. It is not uncommon to see fluorine spelled incorrectly as flourine.
